Salad

Description

A grilled chicken salad can consist of different ingredients and be a healthy food option for you. Salads are both delicious and nutritional. They are usually easy to make and are'nt as time consuming as other foods.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • garlic powder
  • Dried oregano
  • fresh baby spinach
  • red onion
  • bell pepper
  • cucumber
  • grape or cherry tomatoes
  • avocado
  • salad dressing
  • boneless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Combine olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and oregano in a medium bowl. Place chicken breasts in a bowl and let marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a grill to medium-high heat and brush with more olive oil. When ready, add chicken and cook for about 5 minutes on each side, until the internal temperature reaches 165Â°F.
  3. Remove the chicken to a cutting board and let rest while you prepare the rest of the salad.
  4. In a large salad bowl or serving plate, add spinach and veggies.
  5. Slice the chicken and toss it with the salad.
  6. Top with homemade buttermilk ranch dressing, or your salad dressing of choice.
